Scope of work

Four service lines, one standard of clean.

SEC. 01

Post-Construction Cleanup

Rough clean and final clean for new builds and renos — drywall dust, paint overspray, window sticker and label removal, floor prep, and full site detail before handover.

SEC. 02

Commercial Cleaning

Recurring or one-time cleaning for offices, retail, and industrial spaces. Scheduled around your hours, not the other way around.

SEC. 03

Residential Cleaning

Move-in, move-out, and regular home cleaning. Kitchens, bathrooms, floors, and everything in between, handled with the same crew every visit.

SEC. 04

Janitorial Contracts

Ongoing facility maintenance for property managers and businesses — nightly, weekly, or custom schedules with a fixed scope and a fixed price.

SEC. 05

Construction Contracts

Standing agreements with general contractors and builders for cleanup across multiple sites or phases — rough clean, progressive clean, and final clean built into your project timeline.
